FloatBehind automatically pins tips or messages from slack on your desktop.
The app will pin messages on your desktop that team members want you to read.

  1. Let's get started with FloatBehind.

    Download the Mac application from FloatBehind and login with your Slack account. FloatBehind collects shared information and technical tips from your team, and pins them on your desktop.

  2. Assign a command for FloatBehind.

    Assign a command on Slack integration setting page for FloatBehind. Go to Slack integration setting page and set your prefered command name.

  3. Use the command to share your tips with all your teammates.

    FloatBehind will work on every Slack channel. Post URLs that you want to share with your team members by using the slack command. FloatBehind will pin it on your team members desktop.
