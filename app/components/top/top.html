floatbehind.ninja is automatic update of chat tips on your desktop.
He gets the tips that team members want to share in your team and pins on your desktop.

  1. Then, Let's get start with a floatbehind.ninja.

    Please download Mac application of floatbehind.ninja and login with your Slack account. floatbehind.ninja collects shared information like technical tips in your team.

  2. Decide a command for the floatbehind.ninja.

    Please decide a command on Slack integration setting for floatbehind.ninja. Go to Slack integration setting page and set your prefer command name.

  3. Share your tips for your team with the command.

    floatbehind.ninja will work on every Slack channel. Post URLs that you wants to share with team members by using the command. floatbehind.ninja will pin it on your team member's desktop.
